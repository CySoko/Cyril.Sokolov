Refrigerator Defence System

I rebuilt my IDC1 project while taking the class in ATLAS called Object to be more interactive by hacking a taser circuit — replacing the servo with a reed switch, relay, and transistor. After troubleshooting fried components and ill-fitting parts, I got it working with a shocking component, LCD screen, and finished it off with a laser-cut wooden enclosure.
